CIS College
CIS Group is an Educational Group in Lebanon, formed of the following Institutes:
CIS College, Leaders Technology Center, Higher Business College,
CIS College Kurdistan, and Matn University College of Technology.
We are a leading Vocational and Technical Education
College and Training Center that was established in Beirut, Lebanon in 1991.
CIS College also is known for having the most professional Training Center
that offers a wide variety of training courses of different fields.
